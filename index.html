Google Developer
Student Club 

          GDSC BMC is a platform for budding and aspiring developers
          aiming to bridge the gap between theory and practice.
          Our goal is to help them broaden their understanding of
          emerging technologies and shape their skills into a better
          version. The club intends to provide students with hands-on
          technology through Google's curated resources.
